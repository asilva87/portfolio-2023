This is the Vigenere cipher. It is a method of encrypting alphabetic texts. It uses a series of alphabets, with each permutating one letter to the left. It is therefore a Polyalphabetic Substitution algorithm, as it replaces a letter from the original text with another provided by the key.
After the text is input, the cipher, if it has shorter than the original text, is expanded to match it in length. Then a loop is performed for each letter of the original text, until every letter has been replace (encrypted).
